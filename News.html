April 27th, 2021.

Covid 19 forced Rascal Flatts to lose their last tour, as they announced in January of 2020, that the tour indeed was the last for the group. In a recent interview with Jay Demarcus on April 27th Rascal Flatts, the band, may come out of retirement to have their last final goodbye to their fan bases. Demarcus said “I think all of us are at this place right now to where it might not even be a farewell tour — who knows? We may feel compelled enough to go back out and do the music," DeMarcus elaborates. "I'm just speaking theoretically, but [we might consider doing] 10, 15 shows a year, enjoy our Rascal Flatts music along with the other things we have going on in our lives.
